# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling or derogatory comments

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers.
